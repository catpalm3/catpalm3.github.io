About Me

I'm a cartography and GIS graduate student at University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am interested in how cartography and GIS can show infomation in a visual way that text lacks. With that, my goal is to make maps that can educate people without relying on excessive usage of text.

During my free time, I love to do sudoku, read books/comics, and biking.

To get updated with my current projects, go over to Twitter! Or to get more familiar with my background, LinkedIn. As always, you are more than welcome to email me any time at catsapalm@gmail.com.
